My Journey To Choosing BSIT

By: DEMECILLO, Christine E.

Why Did You Choose BSIT?

Growing up, I often envied kids who lived a stable lives, those who could play games freely, without worrying about responsibilities and grades.
My life was different. I faced challenges early on, and was determined to break that cycle. I did not want to be a burden to my Family.
So I took the initiative to apply as a working scholar at the University of Cebu, and fortunately got accepted.

At first, I considered taking BSHM. But when I shared this with our eldest brother, who works as an architect, he reacted strongly and furious.
He questioned my choice, even sarcastically asking, "Do you want to be a dishwasher?"
His words has deeply offended me, and i was stunned, I dont know what to speak back.
But it made me reflect deeply on what path i should take.

I want to be like my brother, because back then my brother had always an interest on taking BSIT
but he was offered a fully paid tuition if he should just take BSNAME.
My brother did not want to waste the opportunity and took the offer.
On the day i got enrolled, i have decided to pursue my brother's dream to dedicate his hard work and sacrifices for us.

When people ask me before, "why BSHM?" i just tell them that I like helping people, without realizing that i can also help people in BSIT.
A memory came back to me months ago before enrolling at UC, one of my gaming accounts had been hacked.
That moment sparked my interest in technology and security.
BSIT was not my first choice, maybe not even my second, but i realized it held the potential to lead me toward a stable, successful future.

Despite how I started, I am committed. I may not have chosen BSIT out of passion at first, but i chose it with purpose.
I am determined to succeed not just for my self, but for my Family and the future I am working hard to build.
